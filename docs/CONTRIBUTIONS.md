## Authorship principle

Authorship should reflect substantial contributions to study conception or design, data acquisition or interpretation, manuscript drafting or critical revision, final approval, and accountability. Repository acknowledgement alone does not establish authorship.

## Dataset and software credit

The repository does not claim ownership of I-SPY2, BreastDCEDL, Zenodo-hosted data, treatment metadata, PyTorch, NiBabel, scikit-learn, pandas, NumPy, Requests, or tqdm. Their original papers, repositories, licenses, and data-use terms must be cited in the manuscript.

## Artificial intelligence disclosure

Any use of generative AI for code review, documentation, or language editing should be disclosed according to the target venue's policy. Human authors remain responsible for the scientific design, code correctness, results, and manuscript content.
